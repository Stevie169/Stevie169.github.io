About MadDining

MadDining is an independent project launched in Fall 2025 by a group of students at the University of Wisconsin – Madison via the MadCoders Club. This website was created to help students and visitors choose dining halls across campus.
If you have any comments, questions, or feedback, feel free to reach out to us via email.
